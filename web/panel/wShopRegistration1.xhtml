Booking
    Important Information:
  • Follow each step to complete your booking.
  • Check the time to ensure that there is no timetable clash.
  • Check your email(UTS Email Address) for the booking confirmation.
  • Cancel, if no longer available, 24 hours before the scheduled session by clicking on the menu button, then the 'My Bookings' tab and then the session you want to cancel.
  • Please note that failing to turn up for your registered workshop is not fair to those on the waiting list. Repeat offenders (2 no-shows) may be barred from registering for workshops.
